---
title: Accelerationism (is cringe)
tags: [politics]
---

* it belies a hubris on the part of socialists -- that we believe our imagined future is so good that its worth sacrificing current lives for, making the current world substantially worse -- but if the 20th century has taught us anything, its that socialists need to avoid hubris at all costs, lest we end up like certain other socialists projects
* its optically really bad, undermining the socialist project -- if people see that socialist's make society worse today, by inducing recessions or whatever, they're not going to support them, and no amount of "we pinky promise this is gonna lead to socialism its gonna be great" is gonna help with that

* i'm skeptical that making society worse will actually lead to socialism -- i think its almost as likely that it will lead to fascism. Bad economic conditions can be exploited for virtually any populist political purpose (hey that's alliteration), and in a country like the US where there's already so much racial and classist animus lurking right beneath the surface, its probably much easier for fascists to exploit populist fervor than socialists
