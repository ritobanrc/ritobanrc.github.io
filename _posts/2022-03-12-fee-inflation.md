## Corporate Greed

When Bernie sanders attributes inflation to corporate greed, he's not saying "corporations are getting more greedy so prices are going up", he's saying "corporations have noticed that they can get away with making more in profits because of the current situation, and they're choosing to exploit that to the detriment of their consumers". It's still reasonable to _blame_ the corporation--they could have _chosen_ to not make more profit. 

More broadly, Libertarians identify problems correctly, they just tend to have strange solutions because they assert that maximizing profit in a market system must be morally neutral (or even morally good), so the blame must fall on the government or whatever else caused the market to get into its situation in the first place, while a socialist like myself would say "the market being in that situation wasn't anything, until the corporation chose to exploit it for profit".
